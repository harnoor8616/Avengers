THE AVENGERS

IronMan

Iron Man, also known as Tony Stark, is a genius billionaire, inventor, and superhero in the Marvel Universe. He dons a high-tech suit of armor equipped with advanced weaponry, flight capabilities, and artificial intelligence. His character is known for his wit, confidence, and determination to use his intellect and resources to protect the world.

Thor

Thor, the Norse God of Thunder, is a powerful superhero in the Marvel Universe. Wielding his enchanted hammer Mjolnir, Thor controls lightning, thunder, and storms, making him one of the most formidable Asgardian warriors. Known for his bravery, strength, and sense of honor, Thor protects both Asgard and Earth from cosmic threats.

Hulk

Hulk, also known as Dr. Bruce Banner, is a superhero in the Marvel Universe who transforms into a giant, green-skinned powerhouse when angered or stressed. As Hulk, he possesses immense strength, durability, and regenerative abilities, making him nearly unstoppable. Despite his destructive power, Bruce Banner constantly struggles to control his alter ego
